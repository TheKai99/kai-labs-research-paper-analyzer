Under the Hood

Built for accuracy

Chunking Method

Your paper is split into focused semantic chunks before analysis. This means Kai processes section-by-section — yielding deeper, more precise insights rather than a shallow overview.

Context-Aware Q&A

When you ask Kai a question, it retrieves the most relevant chunks from your specific paper — not generic AI knowledge. Every answer is grounded in your document.

Structured Summaries

Kai doesn't just compress text — it extracts key contributions, methodology, findings, and limitations in a structured format that mirrors how researchers actually read papers.
